## Recording Checklist

### Before Recording (Do Once)

- [ ] Tripod at chest height, 2–3m away
- [ ] Phone landscape, level (pointing straight at you)
- [ ] Fitted shirt (shows shoulders/elbows)
- [ ] Front-lit (no backlight)
- [ ] Light dumbbell (5–10 lbs) ready
- [ ] Phone video: 30 fps, 720p+

### Per Clip Procedure

```
1. [HIT RECORD]
2. Stand still 1 second (rest frame — CRITICAL)
3. Perform rep 1 (curl up, hold, curl down, pause 1s)
4. Perform reps 2–12 (same rhythm)
5. Stand still 1 second (final rest frame)
6. [STOP RECORDING]
```

**Duration:** ~45–60 seconds per clip

### After Each Clip

- [ ] Rename to naming convention: `clip_{nnn}_{subject}_{view}_{side}.mp4`
  - Example: `clip_001_you_side_right_good.mp4`
- [ ] Move to `tools/dataset_analysis/data/videos/`
- [ ] Add row to `videos.csv`
- [ ] Spot-check: play back 10 seconds, verify framing and no shake

## When to Re-Record

✅ **Do re-record if:**
- MediaPipe missing-person ratio > 10%
- Elbow angle trace has huge spikes or flat spots
- Subject walks out of frame
- Entire clip is dark or backlit

❌ **Don't re-record if:**
- One rep looks slightly off (just label it in quality)
- Small landmark confidence dips (Phase C handles it)
- Lighting is okay but not perfect
